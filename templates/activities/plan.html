Plan Description
Higashiyama Archery Training Hall is an indoor dojo where you can experience traditional Japanese martial arts such as archery, shihan-yumi (four-quarter bow), samurai, and ninja. Utilizing a shihan-yumi (four-quarter bow), which is about two-thirds the size of the large bow used in Kyudo, this is an archery technique based on the fundamental principle of "the target is the enemy," and focuses solely on accuracy in hitting the target. Our archery experience is open to everyone, from children to seniors, regardless of age or nationality. It is also recommended for school trips, and is enjoyed by many students. Please feel free to stop by if you are visiting Higashiyama, Kyoto.
