Refund Policy

At Habaki, we take pride in the craftsmanship and quality of every katana we produce. Due to the custom nature and artisanal value of our swords, we carefully review all orders before production to ensure your specifications are correctly understood.

Part I : Custom Orders
Because each katana is handcrafted to your unique specifications, custom orders are non-refundable once production begins. We encourage you to carefully review your order details before confirming your purchase. If you have any questions or require adjustments before production, please contact us immediately.
Part II : Non-Custom (Stock) Items
For non-custom or pre-made inventory items, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided the sword is unused, in original condition, and returned in its protective packaging. The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs. Refunds will be processed after we receive and inspect the returned item.
Part III : Damaged or Defective Items
If your order arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of receipt with photos and details. We will arrange for repair, replacement, or refund at no additional cost.
Part IV : Cancellation Policy
Orders can be canceled before production begins. Once forging has started, cancellations are not accepted.

For any questions regarding your order or our refund policy, please reach out to our customer service team. We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every Habaki katana.
